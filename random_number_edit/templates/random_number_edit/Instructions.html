Trial phase

After completing your first task, you will now continue with the second task in which you are paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. First we will provide you with an example and then subsequently you will start the second task in this experiment.

One of you will assume the role to be the sender and the co-player will assume the role to be the receiver.Both the sender and the receiver will share {{ Constants.endowment }} lab token. The Sender will decide how much she/he will retain. Then the rest of the endowment will be transferred to the receiver. Prior to making a decision how much to retain, our algorithm will generate a number that determines the proportion of your endowment share. This information is only privately observed by the sender.
