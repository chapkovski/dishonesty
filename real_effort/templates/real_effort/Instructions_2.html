First Task Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. You will be randomly allocated the role of either a Sender or a Receiver. If you are the Sender, you will be required to make a series of decision. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role and given other information relevant to your decision.

This is "the production phase" where you will find a paragraph in on screen. You are requested to transcribe it into an input box in their computer screen; you will have 5 minutes to finish the work. For the correct every word they type correctly on the screen, the participant receives a lab token reward with the value AUD$ 0.05.
