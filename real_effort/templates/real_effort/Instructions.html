Welcome to The RMIT Behavioral Business Lab Experiment Session.

Our session today is a part of a research project “The Experimental study on Making and Communicating Decisions.”

This session will last for 60 minutes.If you decide to withdraw from the project before the session ends, please notify a member of the research team that is present by raising your hand, and we will help you to go through the withdrawal process.

Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. You will be randomly allocated the role of either a Sender or a Receiver. If you are the Sender, you will be required to make a series of decision. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role and given other information relevant to your decision.

At the begining of our session all participants are invited to participate in a production phase where you will find a paragraph in a hard copy format and on screen. You are requested to transcribe it into an input box in their computer screen; you will have 5 minutes to finish the work. For every word they type correctly on the screen, the participant receives a lab token reward with the value AUD$ 0.05 after completing this task, the token is automatically added to participant’s wallet and subsequently referred in the task as participant endowment.
