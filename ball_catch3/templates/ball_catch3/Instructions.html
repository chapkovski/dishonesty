Welcome to The RMIT Behavioral Business Lab Experiment Session.

Our session today is a part of a research project “The Experimental study on Making and Communicating Decisions.”

This session will last for 60 minutes.If you decide to withdraw from the project before the session ends, please notify a member of the research team that is present by raising your hand, and we will help you to go through the withdrawal process.

Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. You will be randomly allocated the role of either a sender or a receiver. You will be required to make a series of decision. Prior to making a decision. You will learn your role and given other information relevant to your decision.

At the beginning of our session, Most of our study participant are invited to participate in a production phase. In this phase you are requested to complete a series of tasks appearing in your computer screen; you will have 1 minute to complete the task in each round. For every point that you receive, you will be rewarded with one lab token with the value AUD$ 0.01. After completing this task, the token is automatically added to participant’s wallet and subsequently referred in the experiment as your endowment. We will begin with a trial page and continue with the first production task.
