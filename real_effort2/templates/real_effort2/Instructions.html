Instructions

In this study, you will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. You will be randomly allocated the role of either a Sender or a Receiver. If you are the Sender, you will be required to make a series of decision. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role and given other information relevant to your decision.

At the begining of our session all participants are invited to participate in a production phase where you will find a paragraph in a hard copy format and on screen. You are requested to transcribe it into an input box in their computer screen; you will have 5 minutes to finish the work. For every word they type correctly on the screen, the participant receives a lab token reward with the value AUD$ 0.05 after completing this task, the token is automatically added to participantâ€™s wallet and subsequently referred in the task as participant endowment.
